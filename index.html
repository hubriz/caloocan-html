Welcome

Caloocan Standard, Inc. (Philippines) is a fully accredited tire dealer that carries the following brands: GOODYEAR, KELLY, BRIDGESTONE, YOKOHAMA, DUNLOP, CONTINENTAL, FALKEN, HANKOOK, GAJAH TUNGAL, NANKANG, PIRELLI, MRF, APOLLO, CEAT, CASUMINA, BKT, DEESTONE, WESTLAKE, DOUBLE HAPPINESS & GOLDWAY tires.

We carry and distribute tires ranging from PASSENGER, SUV, TRUCK, TRAILER, BUSES, OTR, MINING & QUARRYING, FORKLIFT, AGRICULTURAL, FARM TRACTOR and other special tires. We also carry and distribute automotive and motorcycle batteries, with the following brands: MEGA FORCE CALCIUM-CALCIUM & PRIMERA BATTERIES. We also carry and distribute needs for your golf, military, industrial, deep-cycle and UPS batteries.
